## About the Author

**Nik Bear Brown** teaches AI, data science, and applied mathematics at Northeastern University. His courses span from foundational computational thinking to advanced topics in machine learning and visualization. The same instinct that makes contemporary math a useful general-education course — that math is a way of seeing rather than a set of procedures — runs through his teaching.

He is the founder of [Humanitarians AI](https://www.humanitarians.ai/) and the author of the *with LLMs* textbook series. His PhD in Computer Science is from UCLA.

He works in Boston. [nikbearbrown.com](https://www.nikbearbrown.com) · [irreducibly.xyz](https://irreducibly.xyz) · [skepticism.ai](https://www.skepticism.ai)
